## 1. What Is SkySuite?

SkySuite is an all-in-one, AI-powered aviation operations platform for flight schools, flying clubs, independent CFIs, FBOs, and rental operations. It replaces fragmented legacy tools (Schedule Master, paper logbooks, spreadsheets) with a single modern hub for scheduling, fleet management, maintenance tracking, training management, billing, and business intelligence.

**Tagline direction:** "Your flight school, on autopilot." / "The operations platform GA deserves." / "Like having a brilliant ops manager who never sleeps."

**Core promise:** Every club operation — from booking a flight to predicting an engine overhaul — is effortless, intelligent, and reliable.

---

## 2. Target Audience

| Segment | Priority | Who They Are |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Flying clubs | Primary launch target | Nonprofit or equity clubs, 20-200 members, 3-20 aircraft. Currently on Schedule Master or paper. |
| Flight schools | High | Part 61 and Part 141 schools needing CFI management, syllabus tracking, FAA compliance. |
| Independent CFIs | Medium | Freelance instructors who teach at multiple schools. Need student tracking, endorsement logs, logbooks. |
| Rental operations | Medium | FBOs or individuals renting out aircraft. Need scheduling, billing, maintenance tracking. |
| Multi-location chains | Future | Flight school chains operating across multiple airports. |

**Key decision-makers:** Club managers, chief instructors, school owners, operations directors.
**Key users:** Pilots booking flights, CFIs managing students, mechanics tracking maintenance, dispatchers running the board.

| Competitor | Weakness SkySuite Exploits |

## 4. Design Direction

### Visual Identity
- **Dark theme** — the entire application is dark-mode native (deep navy/charcoal backgrounds, not pure black)
- **Premium and modern** — think Linear, Vercel, Arc Browser. Clean, spacious, confident.
- **Aviation-inflected, not aviation-cliché** — no propeller clip art, no cartoon planes, no stock photos of Cessnas at sunset. Use subtle aviation geometry (runways, approach plates, instrument arcs) as abstract design elements if needed.
- **Glass morphism / translucent cards** — the app uses "glass card" components with blur effects. The landing page should share this DNA.
- **Framer Motion animations** — smooth, purposeful motion. Elements should feel alive without being distracting. Parallax, scroll-triggered reveals, micro-interactions.
- **Color palette:** Primary blue (#3B82F6 range), accent emerald for success/health states, amber for warnings, red for critical. White and light gray text on dark backgrounds. Gradient accents.

### Typography
- Clean sans-serif. Inter or Geist. Large, bold headlines. Generous spacing.

### Tone of Voice
- **Confident, not corporate.** SkySuite knows it's good.
- **Technical credibility without jargon overload.** Pilots and ops managers are smart — don't dumb it down, but don't drown in acronyms either.
- **Slightly opinionated.** "Your current tools are holding you back" energy.

## 5. Page Structure — Sections

### Hero Section
**Goal:** Instant clarity on what SkySuite is + emotional hook.

- Bold headline (e.g., "Flight operations, finally intelligent.")
- Subtitle: 1-2 sentences explaining the product for someone who's never heard of it.
- Primary CTA: "Get Started" or "Book a Demo"
- Secondary CTA: "See it in action" (scrolls to product tour or opens video)
- **Hero visual:** An animated/interactive mockup of the app — ideally the dashboard or scheduling view showing the dark UI, role-based layout, and glass card components. NOT a static screenshot. Consider a 3D perspective tilt with depth, or a live-feeling UI animation.

### Social Proof Bar (optional, if available)
- Logos of early clubs/partners, or key stats ("X flights managed", "Y aircraft tracked")
- Can be added post-launch once metrics exist. Placeholder design for now.

---

### Feature Section 1: Smart Scheduling + Roger AI
**The hook:** "Book a flight with your voice. Literally."

Highlight:
- **Roger AI Assistant** — natural language booking ("Book me a 172 Saturday morning"), conflict prevention, optimal time suggestions
- **Voice commands** via Cmd+K command palette with speech-to-text
- **Role-based schedule views** — pilots see their flights, CFIs see their students, dispatchers see the full board, maintenance sees blocks
- **Conflict detection** — real-time, zero double-bookings (database-enforced)
- **Mobile booking wizard** — 4 taps: date, aircraft, time, confirm

**Visual suggestion:** Side-by-side of the Roger command dialog and the schedule grid, or an animation of a voice command turning into a confirmed booking.

---

### Feature Section 2: Fleet Management + Live Tracking
**The hook:** "Know every aircraft like you built it."

Highlight:
- **Aircraft profiles** with full specs, documents (AROW), engine monitoring, analytics
- **Live fleet map** — ADS-B position tracking on a Leaflet map, see every aircraft in the air
- **Engine monitor dashboards** — CHT, EGT, oil pressure/temp trends from G1000 imports
- **Aircraft analytics** — utilization trends, flight mix, revenue per tail, top pilots, day-of-week patterns
- **FAA registry integration** — one-click prefill from N-number lookup
- **Document management** — upload, categorize, track expiration on aircraft documents

**Visual suggestion:** The fleet map with aircraft pins + an aircraft profile card with engine gauges and analytics charts.

---

### Feature Section 3: Maintenance Intelligence
**The hook:** "Predict problems before they ground your fleet."

Highlight:
- **Aircraft Health Score** — AI-computed single 0-100 number per aircraft combining engine trends, open squawks, time-to-inspection, maintenance history
- **Squawk management** — report, categorize, route to mechanic, convert to work orders
- **AD Register** — track Airworthiness Directives, compliance status, auto-link to maintenance requirements
- **Inspection scheduling** — forecast view with horizon groups, conflict detection across fleet, "best days" optimization
- **Work order lifecycle** — create from squawk or AD, track through completion
- **Maintenance forecast** — project upcoming maintenance events, plan budgets
- **Component TBO tracking** — track time-before-overhaul on engines, props, accessories

**Visual suggestion:** The status board showing aircraft cards with health scores (using the circular gauge component), or the inspection timeline/forecast view.

---

### Feature Section 4: CFI Portal + Training Management
**The hook:** "FAA audit-ready. Always."

Highlight:
- **Part 61 and Part 141 compliance** — separate regulatory engines with automatic rule enforcement
- **Digital endorsements** — structured endorsement records with digital signature (no paper)
- **Stage checks** — full lifecycle: schedule, evaluate, sign, CI review. Separate evaluator enforcement.
- **Training syllabi (TCO)** — build curricula, version them, track student progression lesson by lesson
- **Student management** — roster, notes, lesson debriefs, progress tracking
- **Chief Instructor dashboard** — audit readiness, instructor deviations, override logs, compliance reports
- **Dispatch compliance checks** — hard blocks: no valid endorsement = no flight. Non-overridable.
- **Immutable records** — SHA-256 hashing on all compliance-critical records. Records can't be silently edited.
- **Standalone CFI mode** — independent instructors can use SkySuite without belonging to an org, teach across multiple schools

**Visual suggestion:** The endorsement signing dialog, or the chief instructor dashboard with compliance widgets. Emphasize the "audit trail" visual — locked records, signature confirmations, compliance badges.

**The hook:** "Everyone sees exactly what they need."

- **5 distinct dashboard views:** Pilot, CFI, Admin, Maintenance, Dispatch
- **Pilot dashboard:** upcoming flights, currency gauges, weather, recent reservations, fleet status

- **Admin dashboard:** KPIs, revenue overview, fleet health, member management, alerts
- **Maintenance dashboard:** open squawks, work order queue, inspection schedule, hours tracker
- **Dispatch dashboard:** flight board, aircraft availability matrix, schedule conflicts, today's schedule
- **Multi-role support** — a user who is both a Pilot and CFI seamlessly switches between views

**Visual suggestion:** An animated carousel or tab switcher showing different dashboard views for different roles. Show how the same app transforms based on who's logged in.
